The Best Movies According to Robert Swinford

These are the top 3 movies of all time

Movie #1 - Casablanca

In the film, an American expatriate must choose between his love for a woman and helping her husband escape from Casablanca to continue his fight against the Nazis.

Movie #2 - Rocky

In the film, Rocky Balboa, a poor small-time club fighter from Philadelphia, gets an unlikely shot at the world heavyweight championship held by Apollo Creed.

Movie #3 - Star Wars: A New Hope

A young man leaves his home planet to travel across the galaxy and fight the evil empire.
